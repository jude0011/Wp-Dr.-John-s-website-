# Dr. John Oda Website 🌐

This project represents the official website of **Dr. John Oda**, a business strategist, keynote speaker, and mental performance expert. The website was developed using **WordPress** to provide a scalable and user-friendly platform for delivering coaching services, leadership insights, seminars, and business growth resources.

## 📌 Project Overview

The website serves as a digital platform where visitors can learn about Dr. John Oda’s work, access leadership development programs, explore seminars, and connect for executive consulting services.

The platform highlights Dr. Oda’s expertise in leadership psychology, business strategy, and organizational growth. It is designed to communicate professional authority while offering resources that help individuals and businesses improve performance and achieve sustainable growth.

## 🚀 Key Features

* Professional business consulting website
* Built with **WordPress CMS**
* Responsive design for mobile and desktop
* Service pages for executive consulting and coaching
* Seminar and workshop information
* Blog section for leadership and business insights
* Contact and consultation request forms
* Call-to-action sections for business diagnostics and growth assessments

## 💼 Core Services Presented on the Website

The website showcases several services including:

* Executive leadership consulting

* Fractional C-Suite advisory services
* Corporate workshops and seminars

* Online courses and learning resources

These services are designed to help businesses scale effectively while improving leadership clarity and operational efficiency.
